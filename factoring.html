Factoring Trinomial Using AC Method

by Jesus A Acosta

Solve: 6x3 - 40x2 + 56x

Step 1: 2x [ 3x2 - 20x + 28 ] Factor out anything common to all terms.
Step 2: 2x [ 3x2 + (-20)x + 28 ] Write trinomial in staandard form ax 2 + bx + c
Step 3: 2x [ 3x2 + (-20)x + 28 ] Determine product of a·c= 3·28 = 84
Step 4: List all pairs of a·c If a·c is negative, then factors have opposite signs. If a·c is positive, then factors have same signs. Sign of b determines sign of factors. Factors of 84 are: -1, -84 -4, -21 -6, -14 -7, -12 Select factor pair such that their sum is b term = -20 Step 5: Split middle term b order factors as multiple of the a and c terms
